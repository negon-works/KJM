ABOUT ME

Filmmaking isn't just my passion — it's how I tell stories, capture emotion, and bring ideas to life.

Hey there! I'm Kiran, an aspiring filmmaker with a passion for storytelling through the lens of a camera. Ever since I picked up my first camera, I've been captivated by the power of visual storytelling — how a single frame can evoke emotion, how light and shadow can shape a scene, and how a well-told story can stay with you long after the credits roll.

I'm constantly exploring new techniques, experimenting with different styles, and learning from the greats — both classic and contemporary. Whether it's directing, cinematography, or editing, I love every part of the filmmaking process and thrive on bringing creative visions to life.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me watching films (probably with a notebook in hand), brainstorming my next project, or collaborating with fellow creatives. My goal? To create films that resonate, challenge, and inspire.
